Formula 1: Fan Engagement Initiative

Scenario-based. All requirements, stakeholders, and metrics are illustrative, drawn from publicly available F1 fan research and commercial information.
Executive Summary

F1's viewership crossed 1.5 billion in 2023, largely driven by Drive to Survive. But the majority of those new viewers are stuck in a low-engagement state — they watch races, follow a driver on Instagram, and drift. Nielsen Sports research describes a large segment who call themselves fans but watch fewer than four races a year, never buy anything F1-related, and don't engage between weekends. This project — the Fan Engagement Initiative (FEI) — scopes what F1 would need to build to close that gap, approached end-to-end as a BA: problem definition, stakeholder mapping, As-Is/To-Be process analysis, functional requirements, MoSCoW prioritisation, and KPI definition. It's digital-first, targeting casual viewers in the US and India and younger audiences globally.

Process Analysis: As-Is → To-Be
As-Is: Current Casual Fan Journey
1
Watches a race — via TV/streaming after social media exposure. No follow-up prompt.
2
Downloads F1 app — Generic homepage, no onboarding, no 'start here' for new fans.
3
Browses briefly — Content assumes prior knowledge of DRS, tyre compounds, constructors' championship.
4
Returns next race weekend — No continuity from last visit; same generic experience.
5
Engagement drifts — Nothing between events; cold start every time; races in bad time zones accelerate drop-off.
To-Be: Proposed Engaged Fan Journey
1
Onboarding quiz — Favourite driver, team, content format, home market. Personalised from first session.
2
Personalised home feed — Relevant driver news, explainer videos, highlights. No prior knowledge required.
3
Mid-week notification — 'Norris's top 10 overtakes — 4 min watch'. A reason to open the app on a Tuesday.
4
Fan Predictor game — Opens Wednesday before each race. Prediction creates a stake in the outcome.
5
Post-race personalised recap — Within 90 minutes of chequered flag, covering followed drivers and teams.
6
Commercial integration — After 4-6 engaged races, merchandise and early ticket access surfaced at peak intent.
